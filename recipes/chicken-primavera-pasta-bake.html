Classic Chicken Primavera Pasta Bake

chicken primavera pasta bake

This creamy chicken primavera pasta bake combines tender chicken with spring vegetables like peas, spinach, and asparagus. Tossed with cavatappi in a rich cheese sauce and topped with crispy panko and Parmesan, it's baked until golden and bubbling. A perfect weeknight comfort dish!

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces dried cavatappi pasta
  • 2 cups fresh or frozen peas
  • 1 pound asparagus, trimmed and coarsely chopped
